Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
Q1: Which coaching has the best result for IAS in Delhi?
While all institutes listed have great track records, Plutus IAS and Vajiram and Ravi are consistently praised for their high selection ratios. Plutus IAS is specifically noted for its high conversion rate due to smaller batches.
Q2: What is the average fee for IAS coaching in Delhi?
The fees generally range from Rs. 1,40,000 to Rs. 2,35,000 for a complete GS Pre+Mains package. However, institutes like Yojna IAS offer more affordable options starting around Rs. 37,000/year.
Q3: Is 1 year sufficient for UPSC preparation in Delhi?
Yes, with dedicated coaching from top institutes like Vision IAS or Next IAS, one year of rigorous study is sufficient to clear the Prelims and build a strong Mains foundation.
Q4: Can I prepare for UPSC in Hindi Medium in Delhi?
Absolutely. Drishti IAS and Yojna IAS are the leaders in providing Best ias coaching in hindi medium resources and faculty.
Q5: Why is batch size important for IAS coaching?
A smaller batch size, like that at Plutus IAS (70-80 students), ensures your doubts are answered immediately. In larger batches (300+), personal interaction with the teacher is often difficult.
